Немножечко красивых картинок

Everyone can find it difficult to formulate thoughts and feelings. Only talented writers always manage to find the right words, but they also have concise and wise phrases born as a result of creative torments. Excerpts from books can be quoted when the author's point of view coincides with yours or seems interesting. Quotes inspire and motivate, can make you laugh and dispel doubts, distract from the hustle and bustle and push you to action. Fragments of text will not replace reading books, but the phrase will arouse interest in the author and his works. Quotes are a concentration of knowledge and experience, expressed in a single phrase.
